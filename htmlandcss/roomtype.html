Room Options: Choose Your Ideal Space

     HEARSTONE hostel offers versatile accommodation choices to cater to diverse needs. Whether you prefer the privacy of a single bedded room, the camaraderie of a two bedded room, or the communal living of a four bedded room, our hostel provides options suitable for both students and professionals alike.



Single Bedded Room:
  • Ideal for students or working individuals seeking personal space and privacy.
  • Furnished with a single bed, a study desk with a chair, a wardrobe, and possibly additional storage options.
  • Basic amenities such as a reading lamp, power outlets, and possibly a small desk or chair are provided.
  • This room offers a quiet environment for focused study or relaxation after a long day at work.
  • Attached washrooms offer exclusive convenience and privacy, ensuring comfort and ease for residents.
  • Both AC and Non Ac Rooms available
   





Double Bedded Room:
  • Suited for pairs of students or working individuals looking for shared accommodations.
  • Each occupant has their own bed, study area with a desk and chair, and storage space for personal belongings.
  • Shared amenities like study lamps, power outlets, and possibly a communal notice board or whiteboard for communication.
  • This room fosters companionship and collaboration between roommates, suitable for both study groups and professional networking.
  • Includes attached washroom facilities for added convenience and privacy in each hostel room.
  • Both AC and Non Ac Rooms available
   





Four Bedded Room:
  • Ideal for larger groups of students or individuals with varied schedules such as shift workers
  • Features bunk beds or multiple twin beds, study desks with chairs for each occupant, and ample storage options.
  • Shared amenities include study lamps, power outlets, and communal spaces for group meetings or discussions.
  • This room encourages a sense of community among residents, providing opportunities for both academic and professional networking and support.
  • Each hostel room comes with an attached washroom for added convenience and privacy.
  • Both AC and Non Ac Rooms available
