At HEARTHSTONE , we believe in providing you with a comprehensive living experience without any hidden costs. Our total fee encompasses all essential services, ensuring a hassle-free stay for our residents. Below is a breakdown of our total fees:

1. Accommodation Fee:
This fee covers your room or bed space for the entire month. Whether you prefer a private room or a shared dormitory, we offer comfortable and secure accommodations to suit your needs.
2. Amenities Fee:
Enjoy access to a range of amenities included in your total fee. From high-speed Wi-Fi to laundry facilities and common areas, we maintain our amenities to the highest standards for your convenience.
3.Security Deposit:
As part of your total fee, a security deposit is collected upon check-in to protect against any damages or incidental charges during your stay. This deposit is fully refundable upon checkout, provided there are no outstanding fees or damages.
4.Maintenance Fee:
Our maintenance fee ensures the upkeep of the premises, including regular cleaning and maintenance of facilities, to provide you with a comfortable living environment.
5. AC (Air Conditioning) Fee:
Stay cool and comfortable year-round with our AC facilities. The AC fee is included in your total monthly charge for uninterrupted climate control.
6. Mess Fee:
Enjoy nutritious and delicious meals with our mess facilities. The mess fee covers your dining requirements, providing you with a convenient and satisfying culinary experience.
7. Other Services:
Additionally, your total fee covers various other services aimed at enhancing your stay, such as 24/7 security surveillance, access to recreational areas, and more.

At HEARTHSTONE, we prioritize transparency and aim to provide you with exceptional value for your money. With our all-inclusive total fee, you can focus on making the most of your time with us without worrying about additional expenses.
